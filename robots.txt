# WHY there is no Disallow for /alat-gambar.html or /lab.html even though we do
# NOT want them indexed: both carry a `noindex` meta tag, and a crawler must be
# ALLOWED to fetch a page in order to SEE that tag. Disallowing them here would
# block the crawl, Google would never read the noindex, and the URLs could still
# surface in results from external links — the exact opposite of the intent.
# Blocking is for crawl budget; noindex is for exclusion. Don't confuse them.
